Energy Rating Labels (AU)

Many appliances in Australia show a star rating and an estimated annual energy use in kWh. Lower kWh/year generally means lower running costs over time.

Tariffs & Usage

Your cost depends on your electricity tariff (A$ per kWh) and how long you use the appliance. Small daily decisions add up across a year.

Right-Sizing Matters

Bigger screens and high brightness can draw more power. Compare models by kWh/year, not just by features and price.
